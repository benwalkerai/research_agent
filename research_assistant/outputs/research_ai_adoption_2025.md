### Research Notes on "AI Adoption Trends by Industry 2025-2026"

#### Key Findings:

1. **McKinsey Global Survey 2025** (https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai)
   - Current trends driving real value from artificial intelligence.
   - Almost all survey respondents (88%) say their organizations are using AI, but only one-third of all respondents say they are scaling their AI programs across their organization.
   - Just 6% qualify as high performers in terms of enterprise-scale impact.

2. **Stanford HAI AI Index Report 2025** (https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index/2025-ai-index-report)
   - Trends in AI research and development, analysis of publications, patents, and notable systems.
   
3. **MLQ.ai State of AI in Business 2025** (https://mlq.ai/media/quarterly_decks/v0.1_State_of_AI_in_Business_2025_Report.pdf)
   - Adoption is high, but transformation is rare.
   - Only 5% of enterprises have AI tools integrated in workflows at scale.

4. **World Economic Forum AI in Action: Beyond Experimentation to Transform Industry (2025)** (https://reports.weforum.org/docs/WEF_AI_in_Action_Beyond_Experimentation_to_Transform_Industry_2025.pdf)
   - Companies leading in AI adoption outperform their peers by 15% in revenue.
   - Projections show this figure will more than double by 2026.

5. **Bond Capital Trends – Artificial Intelligence (AI) (2025)** (https://www.bondcap.com/report/pdf/Trends_Artificial_Intelligence.pdf)
   - Compilation of foundational trends related to AI.
   - Collection of disparate data points.

6. **Wharton Human-AI Research Gen AI Fast-Tracks Into the Enterprise 2025** (https://ai.wharton.upenn.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/2025-Wharton-GBK-AI-Adoption-Report_Full-Report.pdf)
   - Annual outlook on AI industry adoption.
   - Led by GBK Collective with Wharton.

7. **USDA FY 2025–2026 AI Strategy** (https://www.usda.gov/sites/default/files/documents/fy-2025-2026-usda-ai-strategy.pdf)
   - Goals and objectives to address current state assessment.
   - Path for responsible and effective AI adoption.

8. **AWS Artificial Intelligence Index Report 2025** (https://hai-production.s3.amazonaws.com/files/hai_ai_index_report_2025.pdf)
   - Tracking and interpreting critical trends shaping the field.
   - Shifting geopolitical landscape analysis.

9. **Handshake The Class of 2026 in the AI Economy** (https://joinhandshake.com/themes/handshake/dist/assets/downloads/network-trends/class-of-2026-outlook.pdf)
   - Daily usage of generative AI by 24% of Class of 2026.
   - Comparison to 13% of the Class of 2024 at the end of their senior year.

10. **Protiviti AI Trends and Future Impact (2025)** (https://www.protiviti.com/sites/default/files/2025-02/web_v9_cii-protiviti_ai_trends_and_future_impact.pdf)
    - AI adoption redefining industries with competitive advantages.
    - Financial Services, AI-driven fraud detection.

11. **AI Adoption by Industry 2025** (https://aloa.co/ai/resources/industry-insights/ai-adoption-by-industry)
    - AI adoption by industry jumped rapidly in 2025.
    - 88% of companies now use AI technology in at least one business function, up from 78% just a year ago.

12. **AI Adoption Trends in 2025** (https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/what-we-do/capabilities/applied-artificial-intelligence/blogs/pulse-check-series-latest-ai-developments/ai-adoption-challenges-ai-trends.html)
    - Common adoption challenges for AI trends in 2025.
    - Updated predictions for 2026.

13. **AI Adoption in Enterprise Statistics & Trends 2025** (https://www.secondtalent.com/resources/ai-adoption-in-enterprise-statistics/)
    - Cloud AI platforms dominate at 82% usage.
    - 67% of jobs now require AI skills.
    - 89% expect to adopt generative AI by 2027.

14. **AI and Other Technology Impact** (https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2025/09/technology-impact.html)
    - About 78% of organizations reported using AI in 2024, up from 55% the year prior.
    - Recent analyses found that AI adoption is expected to continue growing.
