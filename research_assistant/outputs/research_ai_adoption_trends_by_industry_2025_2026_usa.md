#### Additional Findings
1. **AI Adoption by Industry 2025** (URL: https://aloa.co/ai/resources/industry-insights/ai-adoption-by-industry)
   - Key Insights:
     - AI adoption by industry jumped rapidly in 2025.
     - 88% of companies now use AI technology in at least one business function, up from 78% just a year ago.

2. **AI Adoption Trends in 2025** (URL: https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/what-we-do/capabilities/applied-artificial-intelligence/blogs/pulse-check-series-latest-ai-developments/ai-adoption-challenges-ai-trends.html)
   - Key Insights:
     - Common adoption challenges for AI trends in 2025.
     - Updated predictions for 2026.

3. **AI Adoption in Enterprise Statistics & Trends 2025** (URL: https://www.secondtalent.com/resources/ai-adoption-in-enterprise-statistics/)
   - Key Insights:
     - Cloud AI platforms dominate at 82% usage.
     - 67% of jobs now require AI skills.
     - 89% expect to adopt generative AI by 2027.

4. **AI and Other Technology Impact** (URL: https://www.census.gov/library/stories/2025/09/technology-impact.html)
   - Key Insights:
     - About 78% of organizations reported using AI in 2024, up from 55% the year prior.
     - Recent analyses found that AI adoption is expected to continue growing.
