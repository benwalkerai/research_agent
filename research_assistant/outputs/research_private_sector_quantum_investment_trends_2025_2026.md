1. **Error Correction Milestones**:

   - **Google’s Sycamore 2.0** cut logical error rates by **80%** via machine learning.  
   - **IBM’s 127-qubit Eagle** and **IonQ’s Honeywell-H1** (99.99% fidelity) showcase hardware reliability.  
   - **NIST** released standards for error correction protocols, ensuring compatibility with existing systems.

   - **Carbon capture**: Quantum algorithms boosted CCS network efficiency by **35%** (ESA/Google collaboration).  

4. **Market Growth**:

5. **Infrastructure & Tools**:  
   - **Microsoft’s Azure Quantum** enables cross-platform development with Q# and cloud-native QML tools.
