1. **NASA's Earth Features**: Confirms Earth as an oblate spheroid with an equatorial radius of approximately 6,378 km and a polar radius around 6,356 km.
2. **Recent Studies (2023)**: A paper from Nature indicates that Earth's shape might be slightly flattened in other regions due to intense gravity anomalies, complicating its overall form.
