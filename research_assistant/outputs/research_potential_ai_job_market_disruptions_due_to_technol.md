By 2026, the landscape of industries and job roles will be significantly transformed by artificial intelligence (AI). The sectors expected to see the most hiring include healthcare, finance, manufacturing, and retail. High-demand AI roles such as Machine Learning Engineers, Artificial Intelligence Engineers, and Research Scientists in AI are anticipated to offer competitive salaries.
