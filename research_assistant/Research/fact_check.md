### AI Trends Overview (2025-2026)

#### **Emerging Job Roles**
1. **AI Prompt Engineer**
   - **Requirements**: 
     - Basic understanding of prompt engineering tools.
     - Elementary programming skills in Python or other supported languages.
     - Domain knowledge specific to the industry (e.g., finance, healthcare).
   
2. **AI Engineer**
   - **Requirements**:
     - Intermediate programming expertise.
     - Strong foundation in machine learning and AI concepts.
     - Sector-specific knowledge related to applications like natural language processing.

3. **Robotics Engineer**
   - **Requirements**:
     - Advanced mechanical engineering skills.
     - Understanding of robotics, automation, and control systems.
     - Proficiency in programming languages like C++ or Python.

#### **Required Skills for AI Professionals**
1. **Programming Proficiency**: Fluency in languages such as Python, Java, or C++.
2. **Machine Learning Expertise**: Strong understanding of algorithms, models, and frameworks.
3. **Data Analysis**: Ability to process and interpret large datasets using tools like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
4. **Critical Thinking & Problem Solving**: Analytical skills for debugging and optimizing AI systems.
5. **Creativity**: Ability to design innovative solutions using AI technologies.
6. **Domain-Specific Knowledge**: Understanding of industries (e.g., healthcare, finance) where AI is applied.
